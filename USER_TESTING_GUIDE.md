## What to Test - Feature Walkthrough

### 1. **Signup & Profile Setup** (5-10 minutes)
- Sign up with a new email OR use the test account
- Go through the onboarding questions:
  - Basic info (name, age, location, gender)
  - What you're looking for (gender preference, age range)
  - Profile details (bio, interests, favorites)
  - Open-ended questions (about yourself, relationship values, etc.)
- Upload a profile photo (optional)

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- Is the signup process clear and easy to follow?
- Are there any confusing questions?
- Should we add or remove any questions?
- Any questions that felt awkward or unnecessary?

---

### 2. **Browse Profiles** (5-10 minutes)
- Go to the "Browse" section
- Swipe through different profiles
- Try the filter options:
  - Filter by gender preference
  - Filter by district
- Click on a profile to view full details
- Try the "Like" button
- Try any other actions available

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- Is it easy to browse through profiles?
- Are profile cards clear and informative?
- Do you like the profile layout and design?
- Are you missing any information on profile cards?
- Would you want to see anything differently?
- Is the like/filter functionality intuitive?

---

### 3. **View Other Users' Profiles** (3-5 minutes)
- Click on a profile to see full details
- Look at their photos, bio, favorites, interests
- Check out the "About" section and other details
- Try the "Like" or messaging buttons

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- Is all the important info visible and well-organized?
- Is the photo gallery easy to browse?
- Would you change the layout or design?
- Are there any details you'd want to see that aren't there?

---

### 4. **Messaging** (3-5 minutes)
- Go to "Messages" section
- Start a conversation with someone or view existing messages
- Send a message

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- Is the messaging interface intuitive?
- Is it easy to find conversations?
- Would you like any features added (emojis, read receipts, etc.)?

---

### 5. **Matches** (2-3 minutes)
- Go to "Matches" section
- View your matches

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- Is it clear who you've matched with?
- Is the layout good?
- Would you want to see anything else here?

---

### 6. **Events** (3-5 minutes)
- Go to "Events" section
- Browse through upcoming events
- Click on an event to see details
- Try to join an event (if you want)

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- Is it easy to find events?
- Do event listings have the information you'd want?
- Do the event categories make sense?
- Would you want to see event details differently?
- Any features missing (like search, filters, calendar view)?

---

### 7. **Notifications** (2-3 minutes)
- Go to "Notifications" section
- Check if you have any notifications

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- Are notifications clear?
- Would you want notification settings/customization?

---

### 8. **Settings & Profile** (3-5 minutes)
- Go to "Settings" or your profile
- Look at available options:
  - Password settings
  - Notification preferences
  - Privacy settings
  - Age range preferences
- Try updating some settings

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- Is it easy to customize your settings?
- Are all the settings you'd expect there?
- Would you want different privacy/notification options?

---

### 9. **Overall Design & Usability** (Throughout)
- Notice the color scheme, fonts, buttons
- How does the app feel to use?
- Is navigation intuitive?
- Does everything feel polished?

**Quick Feedback Questions:**
- How do you feel about the overall design?
- Are colors and fonts appealing?
- Is navigation clear (do you know where to find things)?
- Does anything feel broken or confusing?
- On a scale of 1-10, how polished does the site feel?

---

## General Questions

**After exploring, please answer:**
